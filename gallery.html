A new place,
everything comes at once,
and sometimes it is hard to find your footing.
The hallways feel unfamiliar.
The rooms all look the same.
You open one door and find another.

It is alright to feel lost for a while.
You are with your cabinet.
There is always a way through
even when you cannot see it yet.

And no matter how long the corridor,
spring finds its way in through the window.
It always does.

Thank you for your cabinet.
You are in the spring now, too.
